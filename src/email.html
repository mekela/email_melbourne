The Melbourne Foundation is offering scholarships to current Year 8 male and female students.

Would you like a fully-paid scholarship to a prestigious Melbourneschool in 2018? We are looking for Years 8s moving into Year 9.

The Melbourne Foundation was founded to provide educational opportunities to Melbourne children from financially disadvantaged backgrounds, based on the principle that education provides the best avenue to open opportunities in a person’s life.

In 2018 we are offering a number of scholarships to boys and girls currently in Year 8. Selected students are awarded a fully paidscholarship to a prestigious Melbourne school.
